Our environment is being directly interacted with and rendered using openCV and Pillow as opposed to running through GYM and rendered through pygame like previous versions of our code. Additionally we are using CUDA on the Nvidia GPU.

Our AI's hyperparameters are as follows

  • 512 Batch Size.
  • Input space of 4.
  • Internal Neurons of 32, 32.
  • Output space of 1.
